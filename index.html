About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher working on motor control, decision-making, and learning in the human brain. My research combines behavioral experiments and computational approaches to study how frontal and parietal brain networks support action selection and adaptive behavior.

I am particularly interested in translational applications of neuroscience and in opportunities to bring cognitive science and machine learning approaches into industry.
