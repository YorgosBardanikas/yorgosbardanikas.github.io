Research

What I work on 🧠

Fronto-Parietal Interactions and Motor Anticipation

I analyzed large-scale simultaneous neural recordings from premotor and posterior parietal areas while macaque monkeys were reaching targets of increasing predictability.

Premotor Interactions and Social Uncertainty

I applied information theory to neural recordings from dorso-ventral premotor regions of macaque monkeys, while they were cooperating with a human partner to maximize their reward.

Fronto-cingulate Encoding of Information Gain

I performed Reinforcement Learning modeling of macaque behavior during trial-and-error learning and examined the encoding of information gain in the frontal lobe of the brain.
