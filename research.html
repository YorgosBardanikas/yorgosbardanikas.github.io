What I work on

🧠

Memory & Hippocampal Circuits

Using in-vivo electrophysiology and calcium imaging to map how place cells and grid cells coordinate episodic memory encoding during spatial navigation tasks.

⚡

Neural Oscillations & Decision-Making

Investigating the role of theta-gamma coupling between hippocampus and prefrontal cortex in flexible, goal-directed decision making under uncertainty.

🤖

Neuro-Inspired AI

Translating principles of hippocampal replay and complementary learning systems into more robust and sample-efficient neural network architectures.
